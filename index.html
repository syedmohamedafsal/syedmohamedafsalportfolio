About Me

Code, Caffeine & Crashes: My Flutter Story

Hi, I’m a Flutter developer who believes cross-platform is the future and dark mode should be a human right. I build mobile apps that are smoother than your favorite coffee and crash less than your cousin’s old Android. Whether it’s state management, Firebase, or making buttons that actually respond to taps (yes, I test them), I’ve got you covered. My code is clean, even if my browser tabs aren’t. I love elegant UI, but don’t ask me to design a PowerPoint. I once told a client “Yes, I can do that” — and then immediately Googled how. You bring the idea, I’ll bring the app, memes, and just enough sarcasm to keep the bugs away

FAQs
What types of Flutter apps do you build?

I build cross-platform apps for both Android and iOS using Flutter — including business apps, e-commerce, social apps, and MVPs for startups.

How long does it take to develop a Flutter app?

Development time depends on app complexity — small apps may take 1–2 weeks, while full-featured apps can take 4–8 weeks.

Do you provide backend integration (Firebase, Supabase, APIs)?

Yes, I integrate apps with Firebase, Supabase, REST APIs, or custom backends depending on your project needs.

Will the app work on both Android and iOS?

Absolutely! Flutter allows me to build apps that run smoothly on both Android and iOS with a single codebase.

Do you offer post-launch support and maintenance?

Yes, I provide ongoing support and app updates for 3–12 months depending on the plan you choose.

Can you integrate payments, maps, or push notifications?

Yes, I can integrate advanced features like Stripe/PayPal payments, Google Maps, and push notifications.

What if I need changes after delivery?

I include revisions during development. After delivery, small changes are covered in the support period, while major updates may require a new quote.

Do you offer refunds?

Refunds depend on the project stage. If the project hasn’t started, I offer a full refund. Once development begins, partial refunds may apply.
